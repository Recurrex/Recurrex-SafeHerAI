**SafeHer AI** is a comprehensive, mobile-first web application designed to address women's safety during solo travel. Moving beyond the traditional "SOS button" approach, SafeHer utilizes ambient AI distress detection, intelligent route safety scoring, and automated emergency escalation to protect users even when they cannot physically access their phones.

---

## ✨ Key Features

*   **🎙️ AI Distress Voice Detection:** Automatically triggers an alert upon detecting screams, panic, or keywords (e.g., "help me"), working even when the phone is locked.
*   **🚨 Smart Escalation Flow (Fake Emergency Detection):** If an SOS is triggered and the device stops moving, the system automatically escalates the response from family notifications to alerting nearby volunteers and dispatching emergency services.
*   **🗺️ Safe Route Recommendation:** Maps out routes based on safety scores (street lighting, crowd density, crime reports) rather than just the shortest distance.
*   **📍 Live Guardian Tracking:** Allows trusted contacts to monitor journeys in real time and receive auto-notifications upon safe arrival.
*   **🔒 Emergency Evidence Vault:** Automatically records audio/video during an SOS and securely uploads it to an encrypted cloud vault.
*   **📊 AI Risk Prediction:** Generates real-time safety scores (Safe, Moderate, High Risk) for specific locations.
*   **🚕 Fake Ride Detection:** Verifies cab registration details against official databases to flag suspicious rides.
*   **📱 Shake-to-Alert:** Triggers instant SOS by physically shaking the device 3 times.
*   **🤝 Community Safety Hub:** Crowdsourced, anonymous reporting of harassment, stalking, or unsafe areas to improve the global safety map.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

*   **Frontend:** React (Vite), TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Shadcn UI
*   **Authentication:** Clerk
*   **Animations:** Framer Motion
*   **Deployment:** Vercel (Serverless Architecture)
